Summary Bot

The Summary Bot is an AI-powered tool designed to convert YouTube links and MP4 files into concise summaries. This bot extracts the key points and main ideas from video content, making it easier for students to review and understand the material quickly. It saves time by providing essential information without the need to watch entire videos.

Quiz Bot

The Quiz Bot transforms YouTube links and MP4 files into interactive quizzes. This bot analyzes the video content and generates relevant questions to test students' comprehension and knowledge retention. It offers an engaging way for students to self-assess their understanding of the material and reinforce their learning through practice.
